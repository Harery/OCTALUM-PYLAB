## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency over intensity** - Code every day, even if just 30 minutes
2. **Understand before memorizing** - Focus on concepts, not just solutions
3. **Practice actively** - Write code, don't just read it
4. **Review regularly** - Revisit topics you've learned
5. **Teach others** - Explaining concepts solidifies understanding
